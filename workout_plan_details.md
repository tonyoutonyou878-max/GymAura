# 7-Day Personalized Workout Plan

This plan is designed to provide a balanced, full-body fitness routine, synthesizing methodologies from top fitness applications.

## Plan Overview
- **Goal**: General Fitness & Strength
- **Difficulty**: Intermediate
- **Duration**: 7 Days

---

## Day 1: Chest & Triceps Power
* **Focus**: Pushing movements for upper body strength.
* **Duration**: 45 mins
* **Calories**: ~320 kcal
* **Difficulty**: Intermediate

### Exercises
1.  **Push Ups**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 15
    *   **Description**: Keep your core tight and back straight. Lower chest to floor.
2.  **Dumbbell Press**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 12
    *   **Description**: Lie on bench, press weights up over chest. Full range of motion.
3.  **Tricep Dips**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 12
    *   **Description**: Use a chair or bench. Lower hips until elbows are at 90 degrees.
4.  **Chest Fly**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 12
    *   **Description**: Open arms wide, squeeze chest at the top.
5.  **Plank**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 45 sec
    *   **Description**: Hold a straight line from head to heels. Engage core.

---

## Day 2: Leg Day Intensity
* **Focus**: Lower body strength and endurance.
* **Duration**: 50 mins
* **Calories**: ~400 kcal
* **Difficulty**: Advanced

### Exercises
1.  **Squats**
    *   **Sets**: 4
    *   **Reps**: 15
    *   **Description**: Keep weight in heels, chest up. Go parallel or lower.
2.  **Lunges**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 12 per leg
    *   **Description**: Step forward, lower back knee to ground. Keep front knee behind toe.
3.  **Calf Raises**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 20
    *   **Description**: Lift heels off the ground, pause at the top.
4.  **Glute Bridges**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 15
    *   **Description**: Lie on back, lift hips towards ceiling. Squeeze glutes.
5.  **Wall Sit**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 45 sec
    *   **Description**: Sit against wall, knees at 90 degrees. Hold.
6.  **Jump Squats**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 10
    *   **Description**: Explode up from squat position. Land softly.

---

## Day 3: Active Recovery
* **Focus**: Mobility and light movement.
* **Duration**: 30 mins
* **Calories**: ~150 kcal
* **Difficulty**: Beginner

### Exercises
1.  **Yoga Flow**
    *   **Sets**: 1
    *   **Reps**: 15 min
    *   **Description**: Sun salutations and basic warrior poses.
2.  **Stretching**
    *   **Sets**: 1
    *   **Reps**: 10 min
    *   **Description**: Full body static stretching. Hold each for 30s.
3.  **Light Walk**
    *   **Sets**: 1
    *   **Reps**: 5 min
    *   **Description**: Brisk walk to cool down.

---

## Day 4: Back & Biceps
* **Focus**: Pulling movements for upper body.
* **Duration**: 45 mins
* **Calories**: ~300 kcal
* **Difficulty**: Intermediate

### Exercises
1.  **Pull Ups (or Lat Pulldowns)**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 8-10
    *   **Description**: Pull chin over bar. Squeeze lats at the bottom.
2.  **Dumbbell Rows**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 12 per arm
    *   **Description**: Keep back flat, pull weight to hip.
3.  **Bicep Curls**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 12
    *   **Description**: Keep elbows pinned to sides. Curl weight up.
4.  **Superman**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 15
    *   **Description**: Lie on stomach, lift arms and legs. Strengthens lower back.
5.  **Hammer Curls**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 12
    *   **Description**: Palms facing each other. Curl weight up.

---

## Day 5: Core Crusher
* **Focus**: Abdominal strength and stability.
* **Duration**: 20 mins
* **Calories**: ~180 kcal
* **Difficulty**: Intermediate

### Exercises
1.  **Crunches**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 20
    *   **Description**: Lift shoulders off ground, squeeze abs.
2.  **Leg Raises**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 15
    *   **Description**: Keep legs straight, lift to 90 degrees. Lower slowly.
3.  **Russian Twists**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 20 (total)
    *   **Description**: Sit up slightly, twist torso side to side.
4.  **Mountain Climbers**
    *   **Sets**: 3
    *   **Reps**: 30 sec
    *   **Description**: Drive knees to chest in plank position. Fast pace.

---

## Day 6: HIIT Cardio
* **Focus**: Cardiovascular endurance and fat burn.
* **Duration**: 35 mins
* **Calories**: ~350 kcal
* **Difficulty**: Advanced

### Exercises
1.  **Jumping Jacks**
    *   **Sets**: 4
    *   **Reps**: 45 sec
    *   **Description**: Full range of motion. Arms touch overhead.
2.  **Burpees**
    *   **Sets**: 4
    *   **Reps**: 10
    *   **Description**: Drop to chest, push up, jump up.
3.  **High Knees**
    *   **Sets**: 4
    *   **Reps**: 30 sec
    *   **Description**: Run in place, driving knees high.
4.  **Sprints**
    *   **Sets**: 5
    *   **Reps**: 100m
    *   **Description**: All out effort. Walk back to recover.

---

### Exercises
*   **Rest Day**
    *   **Sets**: 0
    *   **Reps**: 0
    *   **Description**: Take a break. Hydrate and eat well.
